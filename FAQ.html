What kind of file should I give you?

The preferred file type is PDF, but I can work with pretty much whatever you give me (JPG, PNG, AI, PSD, TIF, etc.). Download the template and instructions for more info!

How do I make my own design?

If you don't want to use the template, make sure that your design will fit into a perfect circle, otherwise parts might not be visible.

If you want to be thorough and precise, download the template and instructions here.

You can make your own design with most image editing programs (including MS Paint if you're daring!), but I suggest using a program that allows you use layers, like Photoshop, Illustrator or GIMP (it's free).

Here are some pictures of what the template would look like printed directly onto a button.

It is important to understand that anything outside the circle in the center will start curving around the edge of the button. Also, I will add text on the edge for free.

How does pricing work if I want more than one design?

You pay for the total amount of buttons per order, whether you have 1 design or 10.

For example:
$25 could get you 100 yellow buttons
OR
50 blue buttons and 50 red
OR
You could even get 100 uniquely designed buttons.
The total amount of buttons is the same in all of these cases, so they cost the same amount. There is currently no limit to the amount of designs per order.
